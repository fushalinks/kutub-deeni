The descendants of Nizar bin Ma'add and the parents of Rasulullah (sas)

Nizar b. Ma'add begat three sons: Mudar, Rabfa, and Anmar

Mudar b. Nizar begat two sons: Ilyas and 'Aylan

Ilyas begot two sons : Mudrikah and Tabikhah

Mudrika had two sons: Khuzayma and Hudhayl

Khuzayma had four sons: Kinana, Asad, Asada, and al-Hun.

Kinana had four sons: al-Nadr, Malik, 'Abdu Manat, and Milkan.

Al-Nadr had two sons, Malik and Yakhlud.

Malik b. al-Nadr begat Fihr b. Malik

Fihr begot four sons : Ghalib, Muharib, Al-Harith and Asad

Ghalib had two sons, Luayy and Taym

Luayy had four sons: Ka'b, 'Amir, Sama, and 'Auf

Ka'b had three sons: Murra, 'Adiy, and Husays

Murra had three sons: Kilab, Taym, and Yaqaza

Kilab had two sons: Qusayy and Zuhra,

Qusayy had four sons: 'Abdu Manaf, 'Abdu’l-Dar, 'Abdu’I-'Uzza, and 'Abdu Qusayy;

'Abdu Manaf whose name was al-Mughira had four sons: Hashim, 'Abdu Shams, al-Muttalib and Naufal

Hashim had four sons: Abdul Muttalib, Asad, Abu Sayfi and Nadhla

The Parents of the Prophet (sas)

Abdullah ibn Abdul Muttalib begot the master of all the children of Adam, the Prophet Muhammed prayers, peace, mercy and blessings from Allah be on him and his family.

His mother Aminah bint Wahb ib Abd Manaf ibn Zuhrah ibn Kilab ib Murrah ib Kab ib Luayy ibn Ghalib ibn Fihr ibn An-Nadhr.

Her mother was Barrah daughter of Murrah ibn Kab ibn Luayy ibn Ghalib ibn Fihr ibn Malik ibn An-Nadr.

The Prophet Muhammed (sas) is the most honored of the children of Adam, of the best paternal and maternal lineage, he is the honored, generous, glorified and great.
